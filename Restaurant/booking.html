Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I book for a large group?
    Yes, we welcome group bookings and private events. Please mention your group size in the special requests.
  • How will I know my booking is confirmed?
    We’ll send you a confirmation email after you submit your booking request.
  • Can I change or cancel my reservation?
    Absolutely! Just reply to your confirmation email or call us directly.
